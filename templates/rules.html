Official Rules

1. The Setup

10 cups per side in a pyramid formation. Fill cups 1/3 full with water. Two players per team. The water stays in the cups, you can drink whatever you want (or nothing at all).

2. The Game

Teams take turns throwing ping pong balls into the opponent's cups. If a ball lands in a cup, that cup is put to the side. The first team to eliminate all opponent cups wins.

3. Special Rules

  • Bounce Shots: Count as 2 cups but can be swatted away.
  • Re-Racks: Allowed twice per game at 6, 3, or 1 cup remaining.
  • Island: Once per game, call a specific cup. If hit, count as 2. If missed into another cup, it doesn't count.
